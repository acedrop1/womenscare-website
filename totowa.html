Hours

Parking and access

On site parking lot available for patients. The office is wheelchair accessible.

What you can do here

Our Totowa office offers the full range of services we provide as a practice. Same team, same standard of care as our other location.

  • Annual well woman exams with cervical cancer screening, breast exam, and health counseling
  • Birth control consultation and IUD or implant placement
  • Family planning including counseling, fertility awareness, and pregnancy testing
  • Menopause and perimenopause care with NAMS certified clinicians
  • Endometriosis and chronic pelvic pain evaluation
  • Fibroid evaluation and treatment planning
  • In office pelvic ultrasound
  • Urinary incontinence evaluation
  • Colposcopy and LEEP procedures
  • Sexual health consultations
  • STI testing and treatment
  • Adolescent gynecology for teens 13 and up
