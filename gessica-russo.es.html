Sobre Gessica Russo

Gessica Russo is a concerned y attentive nurse practitioner serving the communities of Paterson, Garfield, y Passaic, NJ. She attended the College of New Rochelle, where she earned her master's degree as a family nurse practitioner. She completed her bachelor's degree in nursing at New York University, y also earned her bachelor's in health sciences from James Madison University.

Ms. Russo is a member of the American Association of Nurse Practitioners. She provides complete gynecological care for women of all ages y treats a variety of medical conditions.

She is a compassionate y dedicated healthcare provider, deeply committed to the wellbeing of her patients. She takes a thorough y precise approach to her consultas to ensure she is consistently giving accurate diagnoses.
